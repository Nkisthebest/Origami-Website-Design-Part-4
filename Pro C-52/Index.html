Interesting facts about cicada :

  • Cicadas have five eyes: two obvious, large, compound eyes, and three ocelli
  • Ocelli are three jewel-like eyes situated between the two main, compound eyes of a cicada.
  • Cicada ears are among the largest of insect tympanal ears with respect to tympanal area (reaching 4 mm2) and the number of scolopidia (up to 2000 scolopidia per ear).
